FindMyAir

Discover Airbnbs that match your ‘preferences’

Motivation

There are several websites like Airbnb that can provide various options for accommodations in the given city based on the cost of the accommodation and a few other filters like rating and proximity to a particular area. However, while looking for a suitable place of stay in a new city, users often tend to visit additional websites to take other factors into consideration like commute time and cost to various points of interests, travel options (public transport/cab service) to various well known areas, and safety of the neighbourhood. Imagine how easy life would be if there exists a one-stop solution that considers all these factors and suggests the most suitable places of stay!

Description

FindMyAir is one such solution that suggests suitable Airbnbs based on the user’s preferences. Besides the price of the room, it incorporates user’s preferences for safety of the neighbourhood and the taxi fares to several points of interest. There is an interactive UI as shown below through which the users can provide their priorities for each of these factors. The ranking algorithm on the backend is based on Multi Constraints Decision Analysis (MCDA) which takes these factors as an input and provides the list of the most suitable Airbnbs.

In a way, this application helps the users make an informed decision about their accommodations during the trip.

Technical Aspects

HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, BootLeaf, Node.js, Python, Flask

Land of Permanent Goodbyes

A Narrative ScrollyTelling Visualization to create awareness about the Syrian Refugee Crisis

Motivation

Are you aware of the fact that the Syrian Refugee Crisis is the largest refugee and displacement crisis in the world? Have you ever wondered about the root causes and the increasing number of refugees during each year? Do you know that there are a few major countries in the world which provide assistance to these refugees?

Description

The main aim of this project was to create awareness among the people about the Syrian Refugee Crisis and encourage them to help and support as much as possible by means of an appealing visualization. Based on the data from UN Refugee Agency, as you scroll throughout the page, the visualization narrates the major events that occurred in Syria in each year and simultaneously shows the increase in the number of refugees by pixels. Each pixel in the visualization represents 10,000 such people. The later part of the visualization introduces the term ‘Resettlement’ and shows the yearly trend of resettlement in different countries.

Indeed, this project harnesses the power of narrative scrollytelling visualization to bring awareness among the people about the largest displacement crisis across the globe.

Technical Aspects

D3.js, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript

E-Voting

Voting at your fingertips

Motivation

Let it be a small-scale election within your club/organization at local level or a major election held across the nation, did you ever wish to exercise your right to vote at your convenience from your home itself? If yes, this application can lend you a helping hand!

Description

This web application helps you to vote as an authenticated person from your PC at your fingertips. The main aim was to build a secure online voting portal and assist the citizens to cast votes from their desk itself at their convenient time during the election phase. The 3 important aspects considered here are:

  1. Security mechanism to ensure authenticity for the real-world presidential elections
  2. Anonymity of vote casted by each user
  3. Simple yet intuitive UI to ease the voting process for all the citizens

Technical Aspects

Java Server Pages (JSP) as a server-side scripting language to create dynamic web pages (based on HTML, Bootstrap and CSS), Java Database Connectivity (JDBC) as an API for backend database connectivity, MySQL for relational database management
